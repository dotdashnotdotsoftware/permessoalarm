Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Any information entered by the user (such as request IDs) is processed locally on the device and is only ever sent to the servers maintained by Polizia di Stato.

The app does not use analytics, tracking technologies, advertising identifiers, or third‑party data services.

The entire source code is publicly available and can be reviewed by anyone.

If you have questions, you may contact the developer using the details provided on the app’s store listing.
